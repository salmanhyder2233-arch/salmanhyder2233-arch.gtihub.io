Andrew Garfield’s Spider-Man Story

Peter Parker is a smart but socially awkward high school student living in New York City. After discovering a hidden suitcase belonging to his father, he begins investigating Oscorp Industries. During his visit to Oscorp, he is bitten by a genetically modified spider, which gives him superhuman abilities.


At first, Peter uses his powers for personal reasons and enjoys the freedom they bring. However, after the tragic death of his Uncle Ben, he learns that “with great power comes great responsibility.” This moment changes him completely and pushes him to become Spider-Man, a protector of the city.


As Spider-Man, he faces powerful enemies like the Lizard while also trying to balance his relationship with Gwen Stacy. His journey is not just about fighting villains, but also about learning who he is and what kind of hero he wants to become.
